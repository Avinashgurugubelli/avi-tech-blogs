## Types of Design Patterns

Design patterns for object-oriented programs are divided into **three broad categories**:

1. **Creational Patterns** â€“ Deal with object creation mechanisms.
2. **Structural Patterns** â€“ Deal with object composition and relationships.
3. **Behavioral Patterns** â€“ Deal with communication between objects.
